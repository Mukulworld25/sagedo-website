Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Google Business Profile?

Go to business.google.com, sign in with your Google account, enter your business details, and complete verification. The process sounds simple but most businesses make mistakes in category selection, business description, and verification that hurt their visibility. SAGE DO handles the entire process for ₹799.

Is Google Business Profile free?

Yes, the platform itself is completely free. SAGE DO charges for the professional setup and optimization that ensures your profile actually ranks in local search results, not just exists.

Can I have a Google Business Profile without a physical shop?

Yes. Google allows "Service Area Businesses" (SABs) — businesses that serve customers at their location rather than at a shop. Consultants, tutors, delivery services, freelancers, and home-based businesses can all create a profile. SAGE DO specializes in setting up SAB profiles correctly so they don't get flagged or suspended.

How long does it take to show up on Google Maps?

After verification, your business typically appears on Google Maps within 24-72 hours. Full ranking optimization takes 2-4 weeks as Google indexes your profile and starts matching it to relevant searches.

My Google Business Profile was suspended. Can you help?

Yes. Profile suspensions are common — especially when businesses create multiple profiles with different emails or list incorrect addresses. SAGE DO has experience with suspension appeals and reinstating profiles. Contact us on WhatsApp for a free assessment.
