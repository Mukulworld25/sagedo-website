### 1. The Core Concept: How LLM Citations Work
The speaker introduces a strategy for dominating AI search engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok). 
- When users ask an LLM a query (e.g., "*What's the best fish food for betta fish?*" or "*What's the best AI SEO software for agencies?*"), the AI generates an answer, recommends brands, and provides **citations/sources**.
- The crucial realization: **Brand mentions and citations are not always the same.** Often, the LLM mentions a brand because it read about it inside one of the cited sources (e.g., a Petco article, a listicle of top tools).
- Therefore, the goal to rank in AI search is to ensure your brand is mentioned within the exact sources that the LLMs are actively citing for your target prompts.

**Step 2: Finding Cited Pages**
